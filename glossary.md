# Acronym and Glossary

- AP         Armor Point

- CHA        Charisma

- CR         Creature

- CON        Constitution

- D6

- DEX        Dexterity

- GM         Game Master

- HD         Hit Die

- HP         Hit Point

- INT        Intelligence

- NPC        Non-Player Character   A character used by the GM to effect action on the player character. For example, to set objectives, provide information, direction and sell Equipment.

- OOA        Out of Action

- PC         Player Character
  
- STR        Strength

- WIS        Wisdom
